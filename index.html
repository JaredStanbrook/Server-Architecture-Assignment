Proposal
The Purpose of this website is to act as a portfolio for my creative work, I’ve chosen this medium to present my portfolio as it allows wide accessibility of my work to anyone who wish to see it, some important needs that the website must address include, it must be able to present my work in an appealing way that makes each piece stand exclusively from one another. The website will provide the ability to contact me through links to my social accounts, as well a description of who I am as an artist. Following W3C web design standards will allow the website to be easily navigated and accessible to anyone, as well as using current design standards to convey to the user my work as being professional and bold.
The design of the webpage with have one landing page, which will be the statement of the whole website to reel in users, on this page links to subpages that present each artwork in its own personal light, while sticking to the overall design of the webpage.
At the centre of the website is its Content Management System (CMS), this will allow changes to the website to be made effortlessly and keep my portfolio current to my latest work I’ve created and to update previous works.
The website has an Attribution 4.0 International Creative Commons License, this means work on this website can be shared and adapted with credit to the original source and be used for commercial purposes as well. This license protects my portfolios work from being stollen, as well I don’t mind people taken inspiration or referring to my work in their own work.
